Contact page

The contact page has four input boxes in it. Consisting of "Your Name", "Your Email", "Phone no." and "Your message". It has its own default value. Email must contain @, Phone no. can only contain numbers, and Names have data type text. If any of the value does not match the data or message will not be sent. Name, Email, and Phone no. must not be blank. To contact us, the viewer can easily access the phone no available on the contact page.
